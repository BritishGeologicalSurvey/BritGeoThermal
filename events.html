BritGeothermal Conference

2nd April 2014, Newcastle upon Tyne

Scope of Conference

The conference programme will focus upon the exploitation of low enthalpy deep geothermal resources in the UK and beyond and is anticipated to include updates upon current deep geothermal energy research with case studies from existing and planned deep geothermal projects.

There will be an opportunity to deliver or listen to presentations, browse poster presentations during refreshment breaks and meet other people interested in researching or exploiting deep geothermal systems. This page will be updated as the conference programme develops.

The confenerce will be held at the North of England Institute of Mining and Mechanical Engineers, Neville Hall, Westgate Road, Newcastle upon Tyne, NE1 1SE which is located to Newcastle Central Rail Station.

Neville Hall - Venue for 2014 BritGeothermal Conference

Who Should Attend?

Anyone currently interested in or researching low enthalphy deep geothermal systems and also anyone interested in investigating the deep geothermal potential of sites within their management or ownership in more detail
